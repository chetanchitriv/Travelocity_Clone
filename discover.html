Discover your World

The world is a really big place, so where you do you start? We'd love to show you the way.

So you want to see the world, but you don't know where to start. That's where Discover comes in. Whether you're planning a year long world tour or just a week in another place, Discover can help you plan and book your next getaway. Even if you have no idea where to start!

There's so much to see and do in the States alone, that it can sometimes be a little overwhelming. Let Discover help you find the best deals on accommodation, flights and rental cars. And best of all, it'll help you plan thrilling vacation experiences too!

Discover the Northeast of the USA

New York City

New York City Yellow taxis move through the streets, dwarfed by the most famous skyline in the world. Clear your palate and take a bite out of the Big Apple.

Washington D.C.

Follow in the footsteps of world leaders and take in iconic monuments, world-class museums, and Japanese cherry blossoms. Welcome to one of the most powerful cities in the world.

Boston

The Heart of the Commonwealth. The Most Innovative Square Mile on the Planet. The Renaissance City. This huge New England metropolitan area has them all.

Atlantic City

Beachfront resorts, a bustling boardwalk, and the bells and whistles of your favorite gaming pastimes are what this quintessential resort town is all about.

Pittsburgh

This town of steel has stripped away its industrial exterior and built itself into a diverse and enjoyable hub. It might be Pennsylvania’s best-kept cultural secret.

New Jersey

While the Garden State has a reputation for being NYC’s suburbs, this state has a thriving metropolis and culture all its own.

Long Island

This real-life setting of The Great Gatsby is home to the rich & often famous. But you don’t need to be a Buchanan to enjoy this offshoot of The City That Never Sleeps.

Cape Cod

Each night the lighthouse beacons warn ships away from the rocky shore, but there’s no reason for you to stay away from this fascinating place.

Ocean City

Towering hotels overlook a shore dotted with theme parks, restaurants, shops and even a golf course, making this narrow strip of sand the ultimate resort town.

Brookly
